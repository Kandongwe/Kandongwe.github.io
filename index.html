2022

Feeding Kentucky Data Normalization

In their mission to help feed many Kenctukians in Kentucky, Feeding Kentucky collects a lot of data about their farmers who contribute to food and other necessary products to the organization. For the organization to keep track of the farmers donating food and selling food to the food banks, they have to collect a lot of data. This project is set to assist Feeding Kentucky with database management. Our primary focus for the project is to help the organization with data cleaning and normalization. As a team, we will work mainly with Docker and MySQL
