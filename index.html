About me

I am a hardworking and driven young man passionate about business, computer science, sports, self-development, and spirituality. My life's mission is to live a life with love, pursue personal development, and experience spiritual enlightenment. Currently, I am studying computer science at Berea College with interests in Human-Centered Interaction, Software Engineering, problem-solving, and UX/UI design. I spend my free time pursuing other passions in music, arts (drawing and painting), writing, podcasting, and sports (calisthenics and track). Education and agriculture have significantly impacted my journey in life, and I am looking forward to investing in these areas in my country (Zambia) someday. Like Martin Luther King Junior once said, "I have a dream!" I have a dream too. My dream is to help create a world where we can all learn, connect, work and grow together despite our differences. I believe life unveils the opportunities we have been given when we choose to leap. Every day is an opportunity to live better. I endeavor to use every opportunity I have to learn, grow, live, love, and serve my community, which is the world.
